Financial Hardship vs Financial Navigation

Financial hardship is a situation in which a person cannot keep up with debt payments and bills or if the amount that is needed to pay each month is more than the amount a person earns, due to a circumstance beyond their control while Financial Navigation is designed to help patients in high-cost drug treatments by providing financial solutions to assist with their out of pocket costs.

Financial Hardship Status in EPIC

  • FRE3 - no longer qualified for FH since patient has been approved previously

  • FH Approved - approved for FH

  • FH Pending - No balance will be sent to collections

Things to Remember for Financial Hardship:

  • Financial Hardship assistance is not available for Cosmetic, Retail Services such as glasses, Hearing Aids and DME.
  • Workers Compensation and Third Party Liability Claims are not eligible.
  • Financial Hardship approvals are based on guidelines set by Federal Poverty Guidelines.
  • Qualified Patients are eligible for a one-time discount up to 100% on their current balance with Duly Health and Care.
  • Charges that have been placed with an External Agency will not be included in Financial Hardship review.
  • Patient will not be able to reapply for this program except for Duly Breakthrough Care Center Patients ( BCC ) and Duly Oncology Patients.

Process and Qualifications whenever the patient is calling to apply or before offering FH.

  1. Check the balance. * Balance should be $250 and up
  2. Check if the account is on PP * If PP is set up, it needs to be remove. Don't forget to inform the patient about it because if the patient is under FH review the account balance will be placed on hold so they don't need to pay any amount until review is done. If payment done while the account is under FH review please be advise that we cannot refund it anymore.
  3. Check if the Patient has multiple accounts. * If yes, check the account if there is a balance and if they are qualified then, apply the other account as well. * Note: Plainfield is EXCLUDED to FH. If the other account is Plainfield don't apply it to FH.
  4. For BH account, before applying to FH check the charges as NSF is excluded for FH.
  5. Educate the documents that they need to submit.
    6.

Reminder for Financial Hardship Application,

If the patient has BH account applying for FH, always check the charges first as FH Team is excluding the NSF charges. So you need to deduct any NSF from the total SP balance to check if the account would still be qualified for $250 SP balance qualification. Unless the patient has other balance from their other account or if this is an Oncology Patient or BCC.

Also, If the account is on FH Pending Status, and the patient is calling about it then make it a habit to check the account for the due date. Because if the patient missed to send all documents needed until the due date then their application will be denied and the FH status will be remove that might result to have the older balance sent to collections.

FH activity should be placed on all account(except Plainfield) and any payment plans on the account should be removed.

Financial Navigation Status in EPIC

  • FN Enrolled - Patient is currently enrolled in a Co-Pay Program or Foundation Assistance for a high-cost drug treatment.

  • FN Review - Patient is on a high-cost drug treatment but may not qualify for a program at this time. Assistance is based on the Drug CPT code and Dx. Foundation assistance is based on eligible funding and diagnoses. Sometimes a patient has an eligible diagnosis but there is no funding at the time of the appointment.

Things to Remember for Financial Navigation:

  • Co-Pay Program: Patients with commercial insurance who are enrolled in a Drug Manufacturer's Assistance Program.
  • Foundation Assistance: Patients with Government insurance (Medicare/Medicare Advantage/Medicaid and Tricare) who are enrolled in a Foundation providing financial assistance.
  • PFC Billed Program Activity: Patients enrolled in a copay program or foundation. Financial Navigation Team has contested the patient's transaction and billed the appropriate program for reimbursement. Qualified Patients are eligible for a one-time discount up to 100% on their current balance with Duly Health and Care.
